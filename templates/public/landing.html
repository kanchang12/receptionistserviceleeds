FAQ

Common questions, straight answers

How does the AI receptionist work?
Twilio routes calls to our platform. Gemini AI processes speech in real-time, generates responses from your business config, and handles the full conversation. Every call is recorded, transcribed, and analysed. Tickets are auto-created.
Will customers know it's AI?
Most don't notice. The voice is natural and context-aware. We recommend transparency: the AI identifies itself if asked directly.
What if the AI can't help?
It transfers seamlessly to your designated number with full context. After hours, callers leave voicemails that get transcribed and ticketed automatically.
Can I keep my existing phone number?
Yes. Set up call forwarding from your existing number, or we provide a new dedicated number in the UK, US, or Australia.
How does caller recognition work?
The system checks the phone number against previous calls. If they've called before, the last 2 summaries are passed to the AI as context for personalised service.
Is my data secure?
All data encrypted in transit and at rest. GDPR compliant. You own your data. Recordings only accessible through your authenticated dashboard.
What happens if I exceed my minutes?
You'll get alerts as you approach your limit. Overage is billed at a per-minute rate based on your plan. We never cut off your service mid-month.
