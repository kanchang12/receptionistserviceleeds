FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How does the AI receptionist actually work?
When someone calls your VoiceBot number, Twilio routes the call to our platform. Google's Gemini AI processes the caller's speech in real-time, generates natural responses based on your business configuration, and handles the conversation. Every call is recorded, transcribed, and analysed automatically.
Can it handle complex questions specific to my business?
Yes. During onboarding, you provide details about your services, policies, and common questions. You can also add documents to the knowledge base at any time. The AI references this information during calls to give accurate, business-specific answers.
What happens if the AI can't help a caller?
You set a transfer number during setup. When the AI detects it can't resolve an issue, or when the caller requests a human, the call is seamlessly transferred to your designated number. After hours, callers can leave a voicemail.
How does caller recognition work?
When a call comes in, the system checks the caller's phone number against your previous calls. If they've called before, the last 2 call summaries (including category and sentiment) are passed to the AI as context. This allows the agent to say things like "Welcome back — I see you called about a delivery issue last time."
Can I use my existing business phone number?
We can port your existing number or set up call forwarding. Alternatively, we provide dedicated numbers in the UK, US, and Australia. Contact us to discuss your specific setup.
What happens when I go over my minute limit?
You'll receive SMS alerts at 80%, 90%, and 100% usage. Overage is billed at £0.08/min. Your AI receptionist keeps working — we never cut off your service mid-month.
Is my data secure?
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Call recordings are stored securely and only accessible through your authenticated dashboard. We comply with GDPR and Australian Privacy Principles.
